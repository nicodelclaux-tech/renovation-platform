5 Pembroke Villas
Interior Design Brief + Purpose 
Statement 
a presentation by Nick
--- Page 2 ---
The goal is to create a timeless and 
effortlessly elegant London townhouse 
that remains true to its classic heritage 
while seamlessly blending contemporary 
design with comfort and practicality
A modern-day family home with a curated 
layered aesthetic that strikes a fine 
balance between classic architectural 
details, natural materials, warm textures, 
and pops of colors to create an interesting
yet lived-in, inviting feel

Understated Elegance
1 4
2 0 2 5
N I C K ’ S  I N T E R I O R  D E S I G N  M A N I F E S T O
✓Invest in well-proportioned furniture with traditional lines and 
comfortable upholstery
✓Prioritize tactile softness — use layered textiles (throws, 
cushions, curtains) to create warmth
✓Use warm lighting: lamps, sconces, dimmers for ambiance
The line is deep, low and luxurious with bold 
rolled arms and an assortment of back pillows
